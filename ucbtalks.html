Manoktok

Avoid opening doors to unknown visitors, especially at night. Manoktok, individuals in black hoods delivering ominous war news, led to the tradition of painting white crosses on doors. As war damaged buildings, including homes, the Manoktok disappeared with no doors left to knock.

The Ghost of Minda Mora

Legend has it that Minda Mora, a University of San Carlos student or actress, was tragically raped and murdered by security guards, and her spirit is said to haunt the USC Cultural Center. The eerie story is heightened by a locked room in the Cultural Center believed to house her vengeful spirit.

Whispers in Room 13

In the quiet halls of UC Banilad, a haunting tale tells of Elena, a former student who took her own life due to academic stress. Now, her spirit roams the deserted classrooms, especially in the locked Room 13. Students share eerie stories of inexplicable sounds, adding a chilling layer to the campus's silence.

Pasundayag

The program serves as a warm embrace to usher students into the new school year, simultaneously paying homage to the celebration of Buwan ng Wika. This annual commemoration, held under the theme 'Dayun Pag-abi-abi ug Pagkahiusa sa Pasundayag sa UC-B,' beautifully encapsulates the essence of unity and cultural appreciation within the University of Cebu - Banilad community.

Intramurals

The annual confetti drop at UC-Banilad is a dazzling celebration, symbolizing joy and achievement. This tradition, coupled with spirited sports events, fosters a festive atmosphere that unites the campus community in celebration. It adds to the vibrant and positive energy of UC-Banilad's annual festivities.
